Color Theory Generator

Choose a starting color and color scheme to generate harmonious palettes using the 60-30-10 rule

How it works:

Starting Color: The color you choose (or we generate randomly) that serves as the foundation.
Generated Palette: Eight colors following the 60-30-10 rule. A design principle where 60% is the dominant color, 30% is secondary, and 10% is accent.

Color Schemes:

  • Complementary: Colors opposite on the wheel - creates vibrant, high-contrast palettes
  • Analogous: Adjacent colors - creates harmonious, peaceful, and calming palettes
  • Triadic: Three colors evenly spaced - creates vibrant yet balanced palettes
  • Split-Complementary: Base color plus two adjacent to its complement - creates high contrast with less tension
